Frequently Ask Questions

CAN I BRING A DATE/PLUS-ONE WITH ME?
Unfortunately, no. We hope you understand that we can no longer accommodate those who are not on our guest list due to limited seats. Please respect our wishes as we're trying to keep our wedding an intimate celebration.

WHAT TIME SHOULD I ARRIVE?
The ceremony will begin at 3:00 in the afternoon. Please arrive at least 15-30 minutes before the ceremony to park and find your seat.

WHAT IF I'M LATE TO THE CEREMONY?
If you are late to the ceremony please DO NOT INTERRUPT, either watch from the distance, wait in your car, or go to the directed reception area. We ask that you do not take a seat as to avoid interrupting the ceremony.

CAN WE TAKE PHOTOS OR VIDEOS DURING THE CEREMONY?
Our ceremony is unplugged and we aim to keep it solemn. All guests are requested to refrain from taking pictures/videos during the wedding ceremony.
After we are announced as Husband and Wife, you are free to take shots. Most importantly, do not block the view of our hired professional photo and video team. We will be sure to share our ceremony photos as soon as we receive them.

CAN WE SIT ANYWHERE AT THE RECEPTION?
NO. It took us a lot of effort and discussion to finish the seating arrangement which is planned for everyone's convenience and network preference. You will be seated along with your group of friends and family.

WHAT TIME WILL THE FOODS AND DRINKS BE SERVED?
Appetizers and refreshments will start after the ceremony. Dinner is set to be served immediately after the couple's first dance. We also prepared some activities for you to enjoy during this period.

DO YOU HAVE ANY PREFERENCE FOR WEDDING GIFTS?
As love is what this day is all about, your presence is something we couldn't celebrate without. We are truly grateful for your time and effort just to be with us. If you wish to give something, monetary blessings as we build our forever home would be very much appreciated.

WHEN IS THE APPROPRIATE TIME TO LEAVE?
We aim to end the program at around 10:00 PM after the Same Day Edit video finishes playing. Please enjoy the celebration and don’t eat and run.

DO WE REALLY NEED TO RSVP?
Yes. This will help us finalize the headcount for catering and seating arrangements. Please confirm by responding to our RSVP on this e-invitation home page. If you responded YES but can't come or NO but has a change of circumstances, please let us know ASAP.

THIS IS A PRIVATE INVITATION
We ask you to keep this invitation private. Our wedding will be a small, intimate ceremony, and only those who are closest to us are expected to be in attendance. Please do not share this with anyone. Thank you.
